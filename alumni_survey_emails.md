# Alumni Survey Emails

Placeholders: `[First name]`, `[school name]`, `[Survey link]`, `[Your name]`, `[deadline]`.
Suggested deadline: **Sunday, July 5** (gives a week from send). A hard date is the single biggest lever on response rate.

Personalization tip: if you know the rating each alum pursued, swap "your training" for "your IFR training" etc. Even one personalized clause beats a mass blast.

---

## 1. Standard (recommended)

**Subject:** 5 minutes to make flight training cheaper for the next student

Hi [First name],

You went through [school name]'s training — so you know it's expensive, and you know it's hard to find out *what* it actually costs before you start.

I'm building a tool to fix that: a public, honest breakdown of real cost and hours per rating (PPL, IFR, Commercial, etc.), so future students walk in with eyes open. To make the numbers real instead of guesses, I need data from people who actually did it. That's you.

The ask is small — roughly the dates you hit each milestone (checkride passed, rating earned). About 5 minutes:

**[Survey link]**

No sales pitch, nothing for sale. Just trying to make this path less of a black box for the people behind you — the same thing you probably wished someone had done for you.

If you can do it by **[deadline]**, it genuinely moves the needle.

Thanks,
[Your name]
